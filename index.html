About Me

Hi there - I'm Ian Maynard. I've been in tech in a number of roles -as an Operator, Programer, Consultant, and as a Salesman. I've since come full circle and enrolled in UNC coding school to reawaken long-dormant coding skills. In my spare time (ha!) I enjoy running, (two marathons coming up), photography and reading. I'm a foodie (graduate of the Cookie Monster School of Culinary Critique, and Deportment) and a HUGE sci-fi and Anime fan - so much so that the thought of Scarlett Johansen playing "The Major" fills me with a curious mix of anticipation, dread, and faint outrage the like of which I havent felt since Starship Troopers came out.
