About Me

Hi there - I'm Ian Maynard. I've been in tech in a number of roles- as an Operator, Programer, Consultant, and as a Salesman. I've since come full circle and enrolled in UNC coding school to reawaken long-dormant coding skills. In my spare time I enjoy running, photography, and reading. I'm a foodie - graduate of the (Cookie Monster School of Culinary Critique) and a Sci-fi and Anime fan - so much so that the thought of Scarlett Johansen playing "The Major" fills me with a curious mix of anticipation, dread and faint outrage. The like of which I havent felt since Starship Troopers was released.
